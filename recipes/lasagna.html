Lasagna

The popular Lasagna!

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps to make Lasagna:

  1. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9Ã—13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  2. Top the noodles with some of the cheese mixture
  3. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan and bake another 15 minutes.
